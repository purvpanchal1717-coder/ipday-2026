🌍 What is IP Day?

World Intellectual Property Day is celebrated globally to spread awareness about the importance of innovation, creativity, and protecting ideas through intellectual property rights such as patents, copyrights, and trademarks.

Intellectual Property (IP) plays a key role in encouraging inventors, engineers, and creators to develop new technologies and solutions by giving them recognition and ownership of their work.

The 2026 theme, “IP and Sports: Ready, Set, Innovate!”, highlights how technology is transforming modern sports. From smart wearables and performance tracking systems to AI-based training tools, innovation is shaping the future of sports.

IP Day 2026 at Government Polytechnic Ahmedabad is organized by the Project & Innovation Lab to promote innovation, startup culture, and technical creativity among students.

Through activities like esports competitions, robotics challenges, workshops, and innovation showcases, this event aims to inspire students to build, innovate, and transform their ideas into real-world solutions.
