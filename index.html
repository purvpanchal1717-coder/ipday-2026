What is IP Day?

World Intellectual Property Day is celebrated to recognize the importance of creativity and innovation in shaping our world. It highlights how patents, copyrights, and trademarks protect ideas and encourage progress in technology, art, and business. This day promotes awareness about the value of original creations.
