🤖 Line Following Robot

Robot Specifications

• Max size: 30×30×30 cm

• Max weight: 1.5 kg

• Fully autonomous (no remote)

• Must be self-built

Track Details

• Black line (20mm width) on white surface

• Track includes curves, turns, and junctions

• Obstacle zones may be present

• Ramp sections may be included

During the Run

• 2 attempts allowed (best time counts)

• Robot can be reset at checkpoint

• No reprogramming allowed

• Sensor adjustment allowed with permission

Scoring

• Fastest time wins

• Incomplete runs ranked after completed runs

Common Rules

• Team size: 4 members

• Only leader communicates with judge

• No external power allowed

• Judge decision is final
